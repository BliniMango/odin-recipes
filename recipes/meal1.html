The Chicago Hot Dog

The first thing every visitor of Chicago should try a chicago hot dog the moment they step into the city. But if you can't make it to the ChiTown, I prepared the recipe here for ya!

Ingredients

  • 4 all beef hot dogs
  • 4 poppy
  • ¼ cup yellow mustard
  • ¼ cup sweet green pickle relish
  • 4 dill pickle spears
  • 1 tomato (sliced into wedges)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 4 sport peppers
  • Celery salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter (for toasting buns)

Directions

Prepare the Hot Dogs:Boil hot dogs for 5 minutes or grill until slightly charred.

Toast the Buns:Boil hot dogs for 5 minutes or grill until slightly charred.

Assemble the Hot Dogs:Place hot dogs in the buns. Add mustard, relish, onion, tomato wedges, pickle spear, and sport peppers. Sprinkle celery salt on top and serve.
