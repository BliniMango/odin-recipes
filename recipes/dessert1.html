Honey Caramel Apple Tart

Inspired by Chicago’s love for caramel apples, this tart uses honey for a deeper, richer flavor.

Ingredients

    For the Crust:
  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup unsalted butter (cold, cubed)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 3-4 tablespoons ice water

    • For the Filling:
  • 3 large apples (Granny Smith or Honeycrisp, thinly sliced)
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • ¼ cup honey
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

Directions

    Step 1:
  • In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and salt. Cut in butter until crumbly. Add ice water, one tablespoon at a time, until dough holds together. Wrap in plastic and chill for 30 minutes.

    • Step 2:
  • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Roll out dough and press into a 9-inch tart pan. Prick the bottom with a fork and bake for 10 minutes.

    • Step 3:
  • In a saucepan, melt butter, honey, and brown sugar. Add cinnamon, vanilla, and cornstarch. Stir in apples and cook until tender.

    • Step 4:
  • Pour apple mixture into the tart shell. Bake for 30 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.
