Honey Caramel Apple Tart

Inspired by Chicago’s love for caramel apples, this tart uses honey for a deeper, richer flavor.

Ingredients

    For the Crust:
  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup unsalted butter (cold, cubed)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 3-4 tablespoons ice water

    • For the Filling:
  • 3 large apples (Granny Smith or Honeycrisp, thinly sliced)
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • ¼ cup honey
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

Directions

    Make the Crust:
  • In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and salt. Cut in butter until crumbly.
  • Add ice water, one tablespoon at a time, until dough holds together.
  • Wrap in plastic and chill for 30 minutes.

    • Prepare the Filling:
  • Melt butter in a pan over medium heat. Add apples, honey, brown sugar, and cinnamon.
  • Cook for 5 minutes until apples soften. Stir in vanilla and cornstarch. Cook 2 more minutes.

    • Assemble & Bake:
  • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Roll out the dough and press into a tart pan.
  • Add the apple filling.
  • ake for 35-40 minutes until golden.

    • Cool & Serve:
  • Let cool before slicing. Serve warm with ice cream!
