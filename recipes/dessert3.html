Chocolate Malt Cake

Inspired by Chicago’s old-school soda fountains and classic malt shakes.

Ingredients

    For the Cake:
  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • ½ cup malted milk powder

    • For the Frosting:
  • 1 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • ¼ cup malted milk powder
  • 3 tablespoons heavy cream

Directions

    Make the Cake:
  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease two 8-inch cake pans.
  • In a bowl, mix flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and malted milk powder.
  • In another bowl, beat sugar, brown sugar, eggs, and oil. Add buttermilk and mix.
  • Combine wet and dry ingredients. Pour into cake pans.
  • Bake for 25-30 minutes.

    • Make the Frosting:
  • Beat butter, powdered sugar, cocoa powder, malted milk powder, and heavy cream until fluffy.

    • Add Malted Balls & Serve:
  • Frost the cooled cakes. Serve with whipped cream or malted milk balls!
