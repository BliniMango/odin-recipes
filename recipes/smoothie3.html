Blueberry Protein Smoothie

A traditional dessert turned into a delicious smoothie drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/3 cup walnut
  • 1 Banana
  • 1 cup carrots (chopped)
  • 1/4 tsp fresh ginger(grated)
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • A pinch of nutmeg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
