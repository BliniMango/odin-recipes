Brown Butter Honey Cornbread Muffins

A nod to Chicago’s cornbread roots with a sweet twist using honey and brown butter.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ cup unsalted butter (browned)
  • ⅓ cup honey
  • ⅓ cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 eggs

Directions

    Brown the Butter:
  • Melt butter in pan over medium heat until golden brown and nutty-smelling. Let it slightly cool down.

    • Make the Batter:
  • In a bowl, whisk cornmeal, flour, salt, baking powder, and baking soda.
  • In another bowl, mix brown butter, honey, brown sugar, buttermilk, and eggs.
  • Combine wet and dry ingredients.

    • Bake:
  • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a muffin tin.
  • Fill muffin cups ¾ full and bake for 15-18 minutes.

    • Cool & Serve:
  • Let cool and enjoy with honey butter!
