Turkey Lasagna

The recipe for a white-sauce based lasagna is great for any leftover turkey.

Ingredients

  • 1 Onion, copped
  • 2 Stalked Celery, chopped
  • One half teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 cups cooked and chopped turkey meat
  • 1 cup cooked chopped broccoli
  • 2 tablespoon butter
